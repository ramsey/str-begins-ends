## [1.0.0] - 2019-06-19

### Added

Polyfill for functions based on Will Hudgins's PHP RFC "[Add str begin and end
functions][rfc]."

* `mb_str_begins()`
* `mb_str_ends()`
* `mb_str_ibegins()`
* `mb_str_iends()`
* `str_begins()`
* `str_ends()`
* `str_ibegins()`
* `str_iends()`
